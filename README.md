# Credit Card Spending Analysis in India  
### Customer Spending Patterns & Financial Insights

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **credit card spending behavior across major Indian cities** using transaction-level data.

The dashboard focuses on understanding:
- Customer spending patterns
- Card type usage
- Gender-based behavior
- City-wise contribution
- Expense category trends
- Seasonal and time-based spending variations

The insights are designed to support **banks and financial institutions** in improving customer segmentation, card offerings, and profitability.

---

## Dataset Overview

**Source:** Kaggle – Credit Card Transactions Dataset (India)

- Total transactions: 26,052  
- Time period: 2013–2015  
- Attributes include:
  - City
  - Date
  - Card Type
  - Expense Type
  - Gender
  - Transaction Amount

The dataset was used for **educational and portfolio purposes**.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

### Power BI
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
- Data modeling
- DAX measures for KPIs and comparisons
- Interactive multi-page dashboard design
- Financial and customer behavior analysis

---

## Dashboard Pages & Analysis Focus

- **Spending Overview**  
  Total spend, transactions, and average spend per transaction

- **Expense Type Analysis**  
  Spending distribution across categories such as Bills, Food, Fuel, Travel, and Entertainment

- **Card Type Analysis**  
  Usage and spending patterns across Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Signature cards

- **City-Wise Spending**  
  Identification of top-performing cities and regional trends

- **Gender-Based Insights**  
  Comparative spending behavior between male and female customers

- **Time-Series Trends**  
  Monthly and daily spending patterns highlighting seasonal peaks

---

## Key Business Questions Explored

- Which expense categories drive the highest spending?
- How does spending behavior vary by gender and card type?
- Which cities contribute most to total credit card spend?
- Are there seasonal or festival-driven spending spikes?
- Which card types present upgrade opportunities?

---

## Key Insights (High-Level)

- Bills and food account for the highest spending categories
- Female customers contribute slightly higher transaction volume
- Silver cards show the highest usage across transactions
- Metro cities like Mumbai and Bengaluru dominate total spending
- Festive and year-end periods show clear spending spikes

*(Insights are descriptive and intended for business decision support.)*

---

## Business Recommendations

- Strengthen city-focused campaigns in high-spend regions
- Introduce tier-based incentives to encourage card upgrades
- Launch seasonal offers aligned with festive spending behavior
- Collaborate with utility and fuel partners for targeted rewards
- Use spending trends to improve customer retention strategies
